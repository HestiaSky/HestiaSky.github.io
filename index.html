PhD Candidate in Computer Science of Tsinghua University

  • Email
  • Github

I am currently a forth-year PhD student under the supervision of Prof. Yong Zhang and Prof. Chunxiao Xing at Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University. Now, I am also working closely with Prof. Xiangyu Zhao from School of Data Science, City University of Hong Kong. I am currently working at Tencent WeChat as a summer intern. In 2022, I have spent 6 months visiting Nanyang Technological University under the supervision of Prof. Cheng Long. I received my B.S in Software Engineering from Beihang University at 2020. My research interests focus on data imbalance problem in data mining applications, including recommender systems, urban computing and medical healthcare.
